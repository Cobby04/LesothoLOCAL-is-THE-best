Our Purpose

The Lesotho Hub was created to connect local farmers with clients, making it easier for them to advertise their products and for clients to find what they need at fair prices.

Our Vision

Fair, healthy and resilient food systems that respect people, planet and future generations.

Our Mission

Enable co-creation and exchange of agroecological knowledge through quality learning videos in local languages using digital tools and innovative scaling models.

Our Values

  • Respect and value for local knowledge and innovations
  • Value for the environment and ecosystems
  • Professional, effective and informed by relevant science
  • Creative, flexible and innovative
  • Sensitive to gender
  • Quality, equality and ethics
  • Passion
